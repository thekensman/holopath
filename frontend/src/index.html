About HoloPath

HoloPath is a free hologram GIF generator that transforms any image, animated GIF, or short video into a stunning holographic animation. Choose from five presets — Classic, Cyberpunk, Ghost, Glitch, and Matrix — and five output layouts for pyramid projectors, LED hologram fans, Pepper's Ghost displays, or direct use. All processing happens entirely in your browser — your files are never uploaded to a server. Learn more →

Supports JPG, PNG, WebP, BMP, animated GIF, and video formats including MP4, WebM, and MOV — all up to 15 MB. Real-time animated preview with instant parameter adjustment. See how it works →
