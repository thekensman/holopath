Contact HoloPath

HoloPath is an independent project built and maintained by a single developer. Whether you've found a bug, have a feature idea, want to share your hologram creations, or just want to say hello — getting in touch is welcome and appreciated.

Bug Reports & Feature Requests

The best way to report bugs or suggest new features is through the project's support channels. When reporting a bug, it helps to include:

  • Your browser name and version (e.g. Chrome 121, Firefox 123)
  • Operating system (Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, iOS)
  • File type you were working with (JPG, GIF, MP4, etc.)
  • A description of what you expected to happen vs. what actually happened
  • If possible, the file size and approximate dimensions of your source media

This information makes it much faster to reproduce and fix problems. All processing in HoloPath runs client-side in your browser, so bugs are often browser-specific or tied to particular file formats.

Share Your Holograms

Created something cool with HoloPath? Sharing your hologram GIFs is always welcome. Tag your creations with #HoloPath on social media, or send them directly through any of the contact channels above.

Seeing what people make with the tool — whether that's portrait holograms, animated logos, nature footage run through the Matrix preset, or experimental art — is genuinely motivating and helps shape what gets built next.

Business & Licensing

HoloPath is free to use for personal and commercial projects. The hologram GIFs you generate are yours to use however you like — no attribution required, no licensing fees. If you'd like to discuss embedding HoloPath in a commercial product, white-labelling, or any other arrangement, please reach out via Ko-fi or Buy Me a Coffee with details about your project.

Privacy

All HoloPath processing happens entirely in your browser — no files are ever sent to a server. If you contact us through third-party platforms (Ko-fi, Substack, etc.), those platforms' own privacy policies apply to any personal data you provide. See our Privacy Policy for full details about how HoloPath handles data.
